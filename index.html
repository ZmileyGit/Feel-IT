What is Feel IT?
book
Imagine that you're a start-up or an enterprise introducing a new product or service. What could you do if you knew exactly how people is feeling about yourself or your new product? That's what Feel IT is all about. We want to provide you with a powerful tool that lets you know the emotions and sentiments reflected in social media about a topic of your interest. We want to provide enterprises, start-ups and consultants with a tool that lets them take the right decisions based on how people is feeling. We want you to know if they feel joy, sadness, anger, disgust or fear.
Why are you making this?
favorite
We love computers, programming, gaming, music, drawing, and many other things, but more importantly we love the idea of empowering people with new technology. We want to make people's life easier with the use of technology. We all have emotions and react according to them, that's why we want to close the gap of communication between consumer and producer. At the core of Feel IT there's an ideal: if we can understand how someone feels about us or something ours we can act accordingly and improve.
Isn't it kind of difficult to know how people feel?
lightbulb_outline
Yes, kind of... You may even ask that if even we, as humans, sometimes struggle to understand each other, how is a computer supposed to do so? Believe or not, they can cope with human feelings with enough training and programming. Consider the following: when we write a tweet or a post we actually reflect emotions with every word we type, don't we? That is where Natural Language Processing enters into scene. If a program is capable of extracting keywords of a text, then it should be able to map emotions to some of those keywords allowing the computer to extract all the sentiments expressed throughout a text. In conjunction with Twitter, a library full of texts about almost anything that people is interested in. That's our plan.

Sounds cool but... Who are you?
people
Ruby, Mike and Erick. That's who we are. Just kidding, we are a team of friends and bachelors of the Instituto Tecnológico de Monterrey Campus Ciudad de México. Our goal is to promote the study and investigation of Natural Language Processing in our native language, Spanish. Currently, this branch of artificial intelligence, computer science and linguistics is having a strong focus in English thanks to digital assistants such as Siri, Cortana and Google's. By providing a Mexican start-up whose goal is focused in this area, we want to boost the development and inversion towards its Spanish segment. We want to do it in such a way that hopefully one day we will be able to provide the same benefits of Feel IT to Spanish speakers.
